About/Team Info
The story of Frankenstein is a widely known story that explores many difficult themes such as consequences of creation, guilt, isolation, madness, and more. The goal of this project is to answer the following research question: How does Victor Frankenstein's mental state deteriorate over time throughout the novel? We will do so by analyzing the themes of words and phrases thrpughout the book and how the themes change over time.
