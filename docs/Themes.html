Themes of Frankenstein

For our project, we identified and marked up reoccuring themes throughout the novel. The main themes we identified were Guilt, Isolation, Ambition, Madness, Secrecy, Consequence of Creation, Obssession, Worry, Self Disgust, Rage, Misery, and Illness. Here you will find links to all occurances of these themes, as well as a written analysis of how these themes relate to the mental decline of Victor Frankenstein
